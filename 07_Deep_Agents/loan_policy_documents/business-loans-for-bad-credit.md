## What is a bad credit business loan and how it works

### What is a bad credit business loan?

These are loans designed for businesses whose credit score or financial history might stop them from getting approved with mainstream lenders. They can take the form of [**secured loans**](/knowledge/secured-business-loans) , [**unsecured loans**](/knowledge/unsecured-business-loans) or alternative products such as a [**merchant cash advance**](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance) .

The lender looks at your overall situation-turnover, cash flow, assets and repayment ability-not just your credit report.

### How does it work?

- You apply with details about your business, accounts and bank statements.
- Lenders assess your application based on more than just credit score.
- If approved, you receive the funds as a lump sum or a flexible line of credit.
- Repayments are made over time with interest, just like standard [**business loans**](/knowledge/business-loans) .

The main difference is that lenders may charge a higher rate of interest, request a [**personal guarantee**](/knowledge/personal-guarantee-business-loans) or ask for security against business assets.

## Loan types available to businesses with bad credit

### Unsecured business loans

Funding based on creditworthiness and turnover, no collateral required. Explore [unsecured loans](/knowledge/unsecured-business-loans)

### Secured business loans

Funding backed by assets such as property or equipment. [Learn about secured loans](/knowledge/secured-business-loans)

### Merchant cash advance

With a [merchant cash advance](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance) , you get a lump sum payment and in return the lender gets a percentage of future revenue, up until the total amount plus fees is repaid.

### Invoice finance

If you work in B2B, you may be used to waiting long periods for funds to be delivered since many industries come with standard 30, 60, and 90 day payment terms. [Invoice finance](/knowledge/invoice-finance) gives you access to those funds earlier.

### Asset finance

There are several different types of [asset finance](/knowledge/asset-finance) . One type enables you to use an asset to gain funding, while another enables you to spread the cost of purchasing an asset. Since the asset is used as security, the lender may require a lower credit score from the applicant to grant a loan.

### Revenue-based finance

Repayments scale with your turnover. [See how it works](/knowledge/revenue-based-finance)

## Can I get a business loan with poor credit?

### Yes - but expect stricter terms. Lenders may:

- Charge higher interest rates
- Offer lower borrowing limits
- Request security or personal guarantees

The good news is that some lenders focus on helping businesses rebuild their credit while still accessing the funds they need.

### Tips to improve approval chances

- Provide up-to-date financial documents and clear cash flow forecasts.
- Offer security, if available, to reduce the lender's risk.
- Show trading stability with steady revenue, even if past credit issues exist.
- Demonstrate how funds will support growth or stability.

### Your business might consider a bad-credit loan if you need finance for:

Your business might consider a bad-credit loan if you need finance for:

- Working capital to cover seasonal shortfall
- Buying or refurbishing premises
- Replacing equipment or expanding operations
- Stabilising cash flow after previous setbacks

Explore our [Business loans hub](/knowledge/business-loans) for insight into different loan types and uses.

## Eligibility criteria - what lenders look for

### Lenders may consider:

- Your business's **trading history** and recent cash flow
- Evidence of improving credit behaviour or fewer recent defaults
- **Realistic exit strategy** if the loan is short-term
- Assets/guarantees you can pledge
- How you'll use the loan and how you'll repay it

## Learn more about business loans for bad credit

### Can I get a loan with a County Court Judgment (CCJ)?

Some lenders accept [CCJs](/knowledge/business-finance-with-a-ccj) if you can show strong revenue or security.

### Will applying affect my credit score?

Yes. Applications usually involve a credit check. Too many applications in a short period can lower your score.

### What's the maximum I can borrow with bad credit?

It depends on turnover, assets and the lender. Secured loans typically allow higher borrowing than unsecured.

### Can repaying a bad credit loan improve my credit rating?

Yes. Making regular repayments on time can help rebuild your business credit profile.

### Are startups eligible?

It's more difficult, but not impossible. Some products, like a [**merchant cash advance**](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance) , may be more suitable.
