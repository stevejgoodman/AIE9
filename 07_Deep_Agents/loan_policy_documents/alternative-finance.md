## What is alternative finance and how does it work?

### What is alternative finance?

Alternative finance refers to funding options that operate outside of traditional high street banks. These include [crowdfunding](/knowledge/crowdfunding) , [peer-to-peer lending](/knowledge/peer-to-peer-lending) , [invoice finance](/knowledge/invoice-factoring) , and [merchant cash advances](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance) , among others.

It provides a more accessible route to funding for small and medium-sized businesses - especially those with limited credit history, irregular cash flow, or who need faster access to capital.

### How does alternative finance work?

Alternative lenders often operate online, using tech-first platforms and alternative credit models. That means applications are usually quicker, with less paperwork, and funding can be available in as little as 24-48 hours.

Repayment structures vary - some are fixed-term, while others (like [revenue-based finance](/knowledge/revenue-based-finance) ) flex based on your turnover. [Business credit cards](/knowledge/business-credit-cards) offer revolving credit for everyday expenses.

Many forms of alternative finance are unsecured. However, options like [asset finance](/knowledge/asset-finance) or [secured business loans](/knowledge/secured-business-loans) may require security, such as equipment or property.

## Types of alternative finance

### Peer-to-peer (P2P) lending

Borrow directly from individual or institutional investors through online platforms. Learn more about [peer-to-peer lending](/knowledge/peer-to-peer-lending)

### Crowdfunding

Raise capital from a group of people in exchange for equity, interest, or rewards. Learn more about [crowdfunding](/knowledge/crowdfunding)

### Invoice finance

Unlock cash tied up in unpaid invoices - includes factoring and discounting options.  Learn more about [invoice factoring](/knowledge/invoice-factoring)

### Merchant cash advance

Receive a lump sum upfront, repaid via a percentage of future card sales. Learn more about [merchant cash advances](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance)

### Revenue-based finance

Repay funding as a share of your business turnover - ideal for fast-growth businesses. Learn more about [revenue-based finance](/knowledge/revenue-based-finance)

### Asset finance

Acquire vehicles, machinery, or equipment without upfront costs. Learn more about [asset finance](/knowledge/asset-finance)

### Business credit cards

Revolving credit for managing short-term business expenses. Learn more about [business credit cards](/knowledge/business-credit-cards)

### Unsecured business loans

Loans that don't require collateral - often based on trading history and creditworthiness. Learn more about [unsecured business loans](/knowledge/unsecured-business-loans)

## Faqs on alternative finance

### What is the difference between traditional and alternative finance?

Traditional finance typically involves banks and long approval timelines. Alternative finance is faster, more flexible, and uses digital processes to assess eligibility.

### Can I get alternative finance with poor credit?

Yes, it's possible. Many lenders prioritise cash flow or business performance over credit scores. However, rates may be higher if your credit history is limited or poor.

### How quickly can I get funding?

Depending on the lender and type of finance, you may receive funds within 24 to 72 hours from approval.

### Is alternative finance regulated?

Yes. Most lenders we work with are authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA).

### Which product is best for a seasonal business?

Options like [invoice finance](/knowledge/invoice-finance) , [merchant cash advance](/knowledge/merchant-cash-advance) , or [revolving credit](/knowledge/revolving-credit-facility) are popular with seasonal or cash flow-fluctuating businesses.
